Our Story

Craft is the
original innovation.

Artisan & Co. was born from a simple belief: the things we surround ourselves with should be made with care, not haste. In 2019, we started as a small collective of three friends who loved handmade goods but struggled to find a marketplace that celebrated the makers as much as the objects.

Today we partner with over 40 independent artisans across 18 countries, bringing their studio practices into homes around the world. Every piece you find here was made by a person, not a machine — shaped by years of craft knowledge and quiet dedication.

We believe in paying artisans fairly, in transparent supply chains, and in goods that last long enough to become heirlooms. When you buy from Artisan & Co., you are not just buying an object — you are sustaining a tradition.

Meet the Makers

Behind every piece is a person with a story. Here are three of our founding artisans.

M

Maria Santos

Ceramics

Maria learned to throw pots from her grandmother in Oaxaca, Mexico. After studying ceramic arts in Mexico City, she returned to her village to establish her own studio. Her work blends pre-Columbian forms with contemporary minimalism.

Y

Yuki Tanaka

Textiles

Yuki grew up watching her mother weave on a floor loom in Kyoto. She studied traditional dyeing techniques including shibori and katazome before establishing her own studio in Osaka, where she blends Japanese tradition with Scandinavian restraint.

T

Thomas Reed

Woodwork

Thomas spent a decade as a furniture maker's apprentice in Vermont before opening his own shop. He works exclusively with locally-sourced and reclaimed timber, believing that the material should always dictate the form.

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make, from sourcing to shipping.

🌿

Sustainability

We work with artisans who use sustainably-sourced materials, natural dyes, and low-waste production methods. We offset all shipping emissions and use compostable packaging.

✋

Authenticity

Every product on our platform is genuinely handmade. We visit each studio, verify each process, and tell the true story behind each piece — including its imperfections.

🤝

Community

Artisans receive 80% of every sale. We provide free photography, marketing support, and business mentorship to help independent makers build sustainable livelihoods.
